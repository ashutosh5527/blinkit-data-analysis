# 🛒 Blinkit Sales Data Analysis | BI Project

## 📌 Overview
Analyzed Blinkit's grocery sales dataset to extract actionable business insights using **SQL, Excel, and Power BI**. The project focuses on transforming raw data into interactive dashboards and meaningful analytics for decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Key Objectives
- Evaluate sales performance and revenue trends  
- Identify top-performing products and categories  
- Analyze outlet performance across locations  
- Understand customer preferences and ratings  

---

## 🧰 Tech Stack
- **SQL** – Data querying (JOIN, GROUP BY, Aggregations)  
- **Excel** – Data cleaning, preprocessing  
- **Power BI** – Dashboard & data visualization  

---

## 📊 Key Insights
- 📈 Identified high-revenue product categories  
- 🏪 Large outlets contribute maximum sales  
- ⭐ Customer ratings vary significantly across product types  
- 📍 Tier-1 locations show stronger performance  

---

## 📈 Dashboard Highlights
- Interactive filters (Outlet, Category, Location)  
- KPI Cards (Total Sales, Avg Sales, Ratings)  
- Visuals: Bar charts, Pie charts, Trend analysis  

---

## 💡 Business Impact
- Helps optimize product strategy and inventory  
- Supports location-based decision-making  
- Enables data-driven business insights
